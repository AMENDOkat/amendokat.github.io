Welcome websurfers!

Hi there! My name is Kat and I’m the webmaster that runs this domain. It’s a hub of everything about me - my artwork, my writings, where to contact me, commission info, fun facts, not-so-fun-facts, my daily life happenings and much more. I built this temple for many reasons, for one, I wanted a centralized space about myself that is my own, without having to rely on the goodwill of my social media overlords to keep my content safe, but also for the reasons that most of you indie web netizens are here, it’s a form of self expression and rebellion against the shit state of social media nowadays. Feel free to have a look around and I hope you enjoy your stay!

  • Digital tomb Published Jan 16 2026 | Last Modified Jan 19 2026

    Dedicatória: Ao verme que primeiro roeu as frias carnes do meu cadáver dedico como saudosa lembrança estas memórias póstumas.

    I like the idea of this place being some sort of tomb after I’m dead. Now, I don’t plan on dying soon, but if I do - if I change my mind or get into some freak accident - this is gonna be… Well it’s gonna be very grim to navigate through that’s for sure. I really like that thought though. It’s like entering a house you know someone’s been murdered in. A constant feeling of dread, always lurking in the air and like something’s missing. I hope this website stays up for a long time after I die. As a memorial of the person I was and a time capsule of that period. Abandoned and frozen in time. Rotting away in some corner of the internet waiting for the domain to expire, much like my own mortal remains rotting on the ground waiting for the maggots to come and feast.
